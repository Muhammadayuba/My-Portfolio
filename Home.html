ABOUT ME

I'm Muhammad a Computer Science student from Kaduna, Nigeria, with a disciplined approach to work and an obsessive commitment to getting things right. At my last job I'd stay up through the night typing exam questions and formatting documents from scratch, not because I had to, but because I couldn't close my eyes knowing the work wasn't done properly.

My interest in tech is rooted in its creative potential. Growing up playing open-world and narrative-driven games the kind that make you stop and ask "how did they build this?" pushed me toward software development as both a technical and creative discipline. I don't just want to use what's been built. I want to be the one building it.

Right now I'm focused on growing into a skilled software engineer with an emphasis on web and application development. But the project I'm working toward is my own startup a platform built specifically for casual poets whose work gets lost in the noise of traditional social media. As a poet myself, I know that gap firsthand. I want to build the space I wish existed one that gives emerging voices a real chance to be heard.
